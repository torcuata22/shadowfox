About Me

Greetings, strange ones! I am delighted that you’ve found my shop. Here there be creepy and fantastical creations, resin trinkets, witchy arts and gothic home goods, as well as magically embroidered bags in which to contain them all! Feel free to lurk in the shadows and peruse. You may even find me there with you, in the untamed darkness.

I live in a little bungalow in eastern Washington with my chaotic wife and our two house goblins, Narcisa and Thorin. When not crafting my wares, I enjoy reading and writing paranormal romance, checking out the latest sci-fi/fantasy films, marathoning anime, playing video games, and adventuring into the unknown via Dungeons & Dragons.

To some, I am known as the Great Hoarder of Tea and dice! To others, I am known as “that scary Goth lady!” But to the strange ones that find me here, it would be my pleasure to be called a kindred spirit.
